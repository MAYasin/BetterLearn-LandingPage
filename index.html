Rising student population

The student population in South Africa is growing at a much faster rate than that of the growth rate of BEd graduates and the rate at which new schools are being built. Shortage of education facilities is looming in the near future.

Increase in drop-outs due to covid

Covid has left many student's dropping out of school due to the space constraints, economic decline as well as job loss as many industries and businesses suffered immensly from the pandemic

Outdated learning & communication systems

The lack of online readiness has been exposed when the South African authorites mandated remote learning to curb the spread of the infectious corona virus disease. Schools were left with no option but to use less than ideal systems such as WhatsApp and d6 communicator.
